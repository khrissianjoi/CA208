
## Haskell List Functions
```haskell
> last [2,4,6,8] 
8
> init [2,4,6,8]
[2,4,6]
> length [2,4,6,8]
4
> null [2,4,6,8]
False
> reverse [2,4,6,8]
[8,6,4,2]
> take 2 [2,4,6,8]
[2,4]
> drop 3 [2,4,6,8]
[8]
> maximum [2,8,4,9,6]
9
> minimum [2,8,4,9,6]
2
> sum [2,8,4,9,6]
29
> elem 4 [2,8,4,6]
True
```
